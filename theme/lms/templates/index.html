We offer introductory and advanced online learning packages for practitioners

Normal Infant Sleep

This programme comprises three self-paced lecture videos, self-test quizzes,
and completion certification. All webinars must be fully completed and quizzes passed at 80% in order to obtain the completion certificate.
You are able to take the quizzes as many times as you need.

Read more

Safer Sleep for Babies

The The Lullaby trust and the Durham Infancy & Sleep Centre research team offer this online course Safer Sleep for Babies.
The training is aimed at health-care professionals whose roles put them in contact with parents, families and parents-to-be,
although it will also be of use and interest to peer supporters, volunteers, and independent practitioners
such as doulas, independent midwives, and breastfeeding supporters.

Read more

Supporting Parents with Infant Sleep

This Introduction to SIDS and Sleep Safety self-study training package is designed for practioners
and others whose roles put them in contact with parents, families and parents-to-be such as
family support workers, peer supporters, volunteers, and independent practitioners such as doulas,
maternity nurses, and breastfeeding supporters.
